From now on Express Bonus is available for all sports events at PlanetaX.


When you are making multi-bets, an additional bonus amount will add up to yout total winning. Express Bonus is available with a minimum bet of Є5 containing at least 3 events with odds of 1.4 or higher.

A bonus is provided depending on the number of events in a multi-bet. In the case of a multi-bet that consists of 3 and more events won, a certain percentage of the won amount is added to the total winning.

Example

You place a 100EUR multi-bet on 4 different events with total odds of 223.14



  • Event #1 - 1.54
  • Event #2 - 8.4
  • Event #3 - 3.45
  • Event #4 - 5


In case you win the bet you receive 5% (1.05) of the amount that you have won, which makes 22.314 EUR ((223.14 x 100) x (5/100)). Your total winning will be 22.314 + 1.116 = 23.430EUR



Total bonus odds will be - 1.05
